# French Toast

## Ingredients

- 6 sliced Texas toast or thick cut bread
- 2 large eggs
- 1/4 cup milk or cream
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- pinch of salt
- splash of vanilla extract
- butter and vegetable oil for cooking

## Instructions

- Soak the toast in the goodies
- Cook those dudes in a pan.
